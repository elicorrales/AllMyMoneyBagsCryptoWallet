Important: Just like you should never write down your login password, you must never write down this extended password either. This password is what generates your seed words and encrypts your wallet backup — keeping it secret is critical.

This wallet is designed for use on your computer only, so we assume you’ve already memorized your login password.

We hope that password is strong enough — we’ll check when you create and confirm your wallet password.

The idea is to use your login password as a base, then add a short, unique wallet word you can easily remember.

Example:

computer-password
           |
           v
computer-password.<wallet-word>
⚠️ Do NOT forget this password! If you are Creating a Wallet, and lose it, you cannot recover your Wallet.

Help Me With My Password

Your password must be at least Good strength to continue.

Try using a short phrase made of a few common words. Example:

        I love my pet
        Try this?

Or use a mix of characters and symbols. For example:

        MySecure!Pass#482
        Try this?

Keep it memorable, but hard to guess. The strength meter above will guide you.

Or extend your existing login password with a wallet word:

my.login.password.<walletword>
